## Can I embed Rover on my website?

**Yes.** You can use Rover on your own websites and applications. The license permits production use for your own products and services.

## Can I modify the source code?

**Yes.** You can fork, modify, and create derivative works. All modifications remain under the same FSL-1.1 license until the Change Date.

## Can I build plugins or extensions for Rover?

**Yes.** Building integrations, plugins, or tools that work with Rover is permitted.

## Can I use Rover to build a competing AI web agent service?

**No.** The license prohibits using Rover to provide a commercial AI Web Agent Service that competes with Rover or any rtrvr.ai product. See the [LICENSE](LICENSE) for the full definition.

## When does Rover become fully open source?

On **2028-03-17** (or the fourth anniversary of a given version's first public release, whichever is earlier), that version converts to the [Apache License 2.0](https://www.apache.org/licenses/LICENSE-2.0).

## What if I need a different license?

Contact [support@rtrvr.ai](mailto:support@rtrvr.ai) for alternative licensing arrangements.
